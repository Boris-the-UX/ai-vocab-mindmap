# AI Vocabulary × UX Research

A practical AI vocabulary and UX research glossary for researchers, designers, and product teams.

The project helps UX professionals quickly understand AI and LLM terms, connect them to research workflows, and navigate related concepts.

## What it includes

- AI/LLM terminology explained in simple language
- UX research applications for each concept
- Branch-based navigation
- Search by terms, definitions, and UX meaning
- Detail panel with related concepts

## Purpose

This is a small personal tool for learning, team onboarding, and experimenting with AI-assisted product building.
